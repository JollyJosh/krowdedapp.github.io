What is Krowded?

Krowded is a crowdsourced, all in one, night life concierge. This app allows uses to monitor the crowdedness of local restaurants, clubs, and night life in real time, using location monitoring services. It also provides users with an ability to see various real time data such as wait times, cover charges, and specials, through user provided feedback.

More Stuff About Krowded

Krowded allows businesses to manage their pages and view statistics about customer traffic. Through close collaboration with the Krowded team, owners will receive actionable data that will enable them to better serve their customer base.
